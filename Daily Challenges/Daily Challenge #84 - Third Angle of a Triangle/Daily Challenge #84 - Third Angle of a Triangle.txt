You are given two angles of a triangle in degrees.

Write a function to return the third angle. Only positive integers will be tested.

Sample Tests:
thirdAngle(30, 60)
thirdAngle(60, 60)
thirdAngle(43, 78)
thirdAngle(10, 20)
