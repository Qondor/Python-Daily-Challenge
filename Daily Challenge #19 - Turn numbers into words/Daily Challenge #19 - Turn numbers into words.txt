Turn a given number (an integer > 0, < 1000) into the equivalent English words.
For example:

wordify(1) == "one"
wordify(12) == "twelve"
wordify(17) == "seventeen"
wordify(56) == "fifty six"
wordify(90) == "ninety"
wordify(326) == "three hundred twenty six"
